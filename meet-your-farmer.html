Meet Your Farmer

(Web series, 2010)

In 2010, we created Meet Your Farmer, a series of eight short films about Maine farmers, for the nonprofit Maine Farmland Trust.

The films show the diverse range of farming operations in the state of Maine. Though some of these farmers are no longer with us, the state of Maine remains a rich and vibrant place in which to celebrate farming's traditions and plan for its future.
